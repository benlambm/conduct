Student Code of Conduct

Classroom Rules

  • Always treat others professionally and respectfully; no harassing, cheating, soliciting, or intimidating behavior.
  • Only service animals are allowed in classrooms.
  • No guests in class, including children; only enrolled students may attend.
  • No playing personal audio/video or private games during class.
  • Phones and wearables must be on silent; notify me before class if you anticipate an urgent call and step outside to take it.
  • No disruptive acts (e.g., chronic off-topic interruptions or side-conversations, or any others behaviors that disrupt the learning environment for others).

Academic Integrity

  • No cheating or unauthorized assistance on any academic work (e.g., having someone else complete work for you).
  • Midterm and final exams are always closed-resource: no AI tools, peers, or external websites can be consulted while taking an exam. This does not apply to non-exam assessments/assignments.
  • Do not reuse the same or substantially similar work from another course here without written permission from all instructors involved.
  • Do not publicly post or distribute instructor-created/led materials, assessments, recordings, or content without permission.

Computers and Facilities

  • Use classroom/lab computers and the campus network only for academic work during class.
  • No smoking/vaping in college facilities.
  • No attempts to bypass security or violate IT acceptable-use/security policies.
  • No theft or tampering with safety equipment; respect college and personal property.
Approved accommodations may apply. Violations may be addressed under the VCCS Student Code of Conduct and/or Academic Integrity processes.
